USAGE for V1:

	Main game is Loto649.

	The system plays a game with X lines (multiple config options) every Mon and Frid for the weekly draws on Thu and Sun.

	The numbers generated by the system are saved in repository (DB or File etc)
			AND
	Then sends an email to subscribers (configs) with selected numbers for this draw.
			AND
	Both the email and when saving numbers must specify for which Draw they are targeted.
	
	Subscribers send the numbers that were drawn by email, in standard format.

	Subscribers will receive a reply if they won enything or not.
	
	Subscribers will also receive, in the same replay, a note if they would have won anything in Past draws.


	OPTIONAL:
		Subscribers can receive daily messages to play for the next Draw.
		Subscribers can send configs by emails.
